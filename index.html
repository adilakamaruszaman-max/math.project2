                        Why do we need the Derivative in Food & Agro-Tech?
                    

                    

                        In commercial oyster mushroom farms across Selangor and Johor, resource utilization is vital. Looking only at the total weight curve $f(x)$ tells you how much crop you have, but not how fast it is biological compounding. 
                    

                    

                        By mapping the first derivative $f'(x)$, food processing factories can target the absolute peak day of crop acceleration. This maximum rate allows processors to optimize downstream logistics: arranging refrigeration, allocating processing lab labor, and calculating dehydration schedules exactly when the growth rate is maximal.
